# thesis: crypto-analytics

## core problem
a serious crypto trader opens their day needing to answer one question: is the current setup risk-on or risk-off? the data exists across five sources. synthesizing it manually takes 45 minutes. most traders skip it, then get caught by a funding squeeze or on-chain distribution signal they already had the data for.

## what we do
deliver a morning setup brief before the European session opens. scored signals across derivatives, on-chain, and sentiment. one clear tone: risk-on / neutral / risk-off, with the flags that drove it.

## the wedge
BTC and ETH only to start. most retail traders size everything relative to BTC. nail the daily BTC/ETH brief before expanding to alts.

## why now
on-chain data apis are mature and affordable. the bottleneck is not access — it is synthesis at the speed the market moves. agents handle the synthesis layer.

## what we are not
- we are not a trading bot or execution layer
- we do not manage funds or connect to exchange accounts
- we do not provide financial advice or regulated signals
