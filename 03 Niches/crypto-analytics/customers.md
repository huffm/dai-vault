# customers: crypto-analytics

## primary customer (v1)
active retail traders who already pay for Glassnode or CoinGlass — they know on-chain signals matter but don't have 45 minutes every morning to check five dashboards. they want the synthesis, not more raw data.

## secondary customers
- small crypto funds (2-10 person teams) who want a morning briefing they don't have to build themselves
- crypto analysts who want a structured signal layer to sanity-check their own thesis before posting

## what they pay for today
- paid signal channels on telegram/discord — high noise, low accountability
- on-chain data platforms (glassnode, nansen) — raw data, no synthesis
- crypto media (the block, coindesk) — narrative journalism, not decision-ready

## what they actually need
- a consistent format they can evaluate over time
- signal scoring they can trust over weeks, not one-off calls
- a structured way to separate real setups from noise

## acquisition assumption
communities: crypto twitter/x, r/cryptocurrency, telegram trading groups
reach them with free daily brief samples, convert with depth + alert tier
