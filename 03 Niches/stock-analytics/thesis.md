# thesis: stock-analytics

## core problem
most retail investors get blindsided into earnings — not because the data wasn't available, but because they had no systematic way to assess the setup in the 48 hours before. they show up to an earnings event without knowing whether estimates have been revised down, whether insiders have been selling, or whether the technical structure is overextended. they lose not from bad thesis but from bad preparation.

## what we do
surface structured stock briefs that score a watchlist against current signal conditions — earnings cycle, technical structure, insider activity, and macro backdrop.

## the wedge
a pre-earnings brief for a small watchlist that tells an investor whether the setup into earnings looks favorable or stacked against them. one decision moment, well-covered.

## why now
sec edgar, polygon, and earnings calendar apis are mature and affordable. the synthesis gap is the bottleneck. agents close it.

## what we are not
- we are not a robo-advisor or managed account platform
- we do not execute trades or connect to brokerage accounts
- we do not provide regulated investment advice
