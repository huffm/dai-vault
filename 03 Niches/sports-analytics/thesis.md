# thesis: sports-analytics

## core problem
lines open days before a game. sharp money moves them within hours. by the time a serious recreational bettor manually checks injuries, weather, and line movement, the edge is already gone or the window has narrowed.

## what we do
deliver a scored pre-game brief before the line settles. not picks — signal context that tells a bettor whether the current spread reflects the full picture or not.

## the wedge
NFL and NBA in the first slice. NFL for its weekly cadence, manageable volume, and the most liquid betting market in the US. NBA for its nightly game volume, long season, and year-round coverage that keeps the product active outside of football season. soccer and other sports are later expansion — not part of v1.

## why now
odds apis, injury feeds, and line movement data are all accessible and affordable. the gap is synthesis speed. agents close it in seconds instead of hours.

## what we are not
- we are not a tout service or pick-seller
- we are not a fantasy platform
- we do not place bets or integrate with books
