# value proposition: sports-v1

## core value
get the signal picture on every NFL game before the line settles — in two minutes, not two hours.

## what it replaces
- manually checking odds sites, injury wires, and weather forecasts before each game
- paying a tout for a pick with no reasoning attached
- reading beat writer coverage that is qualitative and never decision-ready

## why it is better than alternatives
| alternative | weakness | our difference |
|---|---|---|
| tout picks | no transparency, no reasoning | we show the signals, not just the call |
| manual research | takes 1-2 hours per game | brief is ready, scored, and waiting |
| beat writer content | narrative, not decision-ready | structured signal table with a clear aggregate score |

## what makes it sticky
- consistent format means users can evaluate accuracy over time
- line movement re-alert (pro) means they get notified when the picture changes
- trust is built game by game, week by week — not from one headline

## one-liner for acquisition
"stop guessing. see the signals."
