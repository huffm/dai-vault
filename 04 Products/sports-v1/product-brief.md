# product brief: sports-v1

## what it is
a pre-game intelligence brief for NFL and NBA bettors. delivered before the betting window tightens, scored across five signal categories, and formatted for a decision — not entertainment.

## the product in one sentence
a signal-scored game brief for NFL and NBA that tells a sharp recreational bettor whether the current spread reflects the full picture, delivered before they need to act.

## what it is not
- not a pick service
- not a fantasy tool
- not a real-time in-game product

## v1 sports
NFL and NBA. soccer, MLB, NHL, and college sports are later expansion — not part of v1.

## delivery
email (starter), slack/webhook (pro). brief arrives morning of game day for same-week games, 24 hours before kickoff for early-week scheduling.

## status
pre-build. niche thesis validated. data sources identified. workflow design ready.
