# target customer: sports-v1

## primary
recreational bettors who take it seriously — people betting $500–$5000/week who do their own research but are dissatisfied with the tout model. they already pay $30–$100/month for picks and want something more transparent.

## what they look like
- bet NFL and/or NBA regularly
- check injury reports and line movement manually today
- have tried a pick service and found it low-accountability
- want context to make their own call, not someone else's conclusion

## first conversion trigger
they see a free NFL brief, check whether the signals match what they already believed, find one thing they missed, and decide it's worth paying for full NFL coverage plus NBA access.

## secondary
- DFS players who need lineup context before lock
- sports media writers who want structured game previews without manual research

## who this is not for
- casual bettors who bet for fun and don't research
- professional syndicates who build their own models
