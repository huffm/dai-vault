# product brief: crypto-v1

## what it is
a daily morning setup brief for active crypto traders. delivered before the European session opens, scored across derivatives, on-chain, and sentiment signals, with one clear output: is the current setup risk-on, neutral, or risk-off?

## the product in one sentence
a signal-scored morning brief for BTC and ETH that replaces 45 minutes of manual dashboard-checking with a two-minute read before you trade.

## what it is not
- not a trading bot or execution layer
- not a portfolio tracker
- not a financial advisor or regulated signal service

## v1 assets
BTC and ETH only. watchlist expansion available in paid tiers.

## delivery
email (starter), slack/webhook (pro). brief delivered at 06:00 UTC. funding rate extreme alert delivered on-demand when threshold is breached.

## status
pre-build. niche thesis validated. data sources identified. workflow design ready.
