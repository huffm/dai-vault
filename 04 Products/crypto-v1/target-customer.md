# target customer: crypto-v1

## primary
active retail crypto traders who already pay for Glassnode or CoinGlass. they know on-chain and derivatives signals matter — they just don't have 45 minutes every morning to synthesize them before the session opens. they want the interpretation, not more raw data.

## what they look like
- trade BTC and ETH with conviction, not just hold
- already aware of funding rates, on-chain flows, and fear/greed
- check multiple data sources manually but inconsistently
- have been caught off-guard by a funding squeeze or distribution event they had the data for

## first conversion trigger
they read the free BTC brief, find it matches or improves on their own morning read, and decide the time savings alone are worth $29/month.

## secondary
- small crypto funds (2-10 person teams) who want a morning briefing they don't have to build themselves
- crypto analysts who want a signal layer to sanity-check their thesis before posting publicly

## who this is not for
- casual holders who don't trade actively
- DeFi-native users focused on protocol-level activity
- traders who want automated execution
