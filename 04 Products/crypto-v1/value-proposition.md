# value proposition: crypto-v1

## core value
know the setup before the session opens — without spending your morning checking five dashboards.

## what it replaces
- manual Glassnode, CoinGlass, and CoinGecko checks every morning
- paid telegram signal channels with no reasoning and no accountability
- reading crypto media that is narrative, not signal-scored

## why it is better than alternatives
| alternative | weakness | our difference |
|---|---|---|
| glassnode / coinglass | raw data, no synthesis | we score and interpret, not just display |
| telegram signal channels | low trust, no track record | structured scoring format you can evaluate over time |
| crypto media | narrative journalism | signal table with aggregate tone: risk-on / neutral / risk-off |

## what makes it sticky
- consistent format means users can track whether the signal scoring predicts market behavior
- funding rate alert means they get notified on the one intraday event most likely to matter
- the brief is calibration tool and time-saver in one — value compounds with use

## one-liner for acquisition
"the morning crypto brief for traders who already know what to look for."
