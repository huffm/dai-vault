# product brief: kalshi-v1

## what it is
a pre-event intelligence brief for Kalshi traders focused on economic data markets. delivered 24 hours before a scheduled economic release, it compares the current Kalshi market price to every available reference probability — CME futures, economic consensus, model forecasts — and flags whether a contract looks mispriced.

## the product in one sentence
a scored pre-event brief for Kalshi economic markets that assembles the reference probability picture traders are doing manually and tells them where the crowd looks wrong.

## what it is not
- not a trading bot or automated execution layer
- not a guarantee of resolution outcomes
- not a substitute for regulated financial advice

## v1 market categories
economic data releases only: CPI, nonfarm payrolls, FOMC rate decisions. expand to political and weather markets post-validation.

## delivery
email (starter), slack/webhook (pro). pre-event brief triggers 24 hours before each major scheduled release. price movement alert on significant intraday change (pro only).

## tam note
kalshi's active US trader base is small today. treat this product as an experiment. target: 50 paying users within 60 days of launch. if not reached, revisit as a feature of another product rather than a standalone subscription.

## status
pre-build. niche thesis validated. data sources identified. workflow design ready.
