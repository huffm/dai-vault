# product brief: stock-v1

## what it is
a pre-earnings intelligence brief for stock analysts and newsletter operators. delivered 48 hours before an earnings event, scored across fundamentals, technical structure, insider activity, and macro backdrop. built for people who need to cover a watchlist, not just one stock.

## the product in one sentence
a signal-scored pre-earnings brief for a custom watchlist that gives analysts and newsletter writers the structured context they need before a catalyst — without the manual research.

## what it is not
- not a robo-advisor or portfolio manager
- not a trading platform or order execution layer
- not a substitute for regulated investment advice

## v1 watchlist size
up to 3 stocks (free), 10 stocks (starter), 50 stocks (pro/RIA).

## delivery
email (starter), slack/webhook (pro). earnings brief triggers 48 hours before each event. weekly watchlist brief delivered Sunday evening.

## status
pre-build. niche thesis validated. data sources identified. workflow design ready.
