# value proposition: stock-v1

## core value
show up to every earnings event prepared — with the signal picture already assembled, not assembled by you the morning of.

## what it replaces
- manually checking SEC EDGAR, Polygon data, analyst estimate pages, and fintwit noise before each earnings call
- Seeking Alpha premium or Motley Fool — opinion-heavy, not signal-scored
- the quarterly scramble to write something coherent about a stock you haven't fully checked

## why it is better than alternatives
| alternative | weakness | our difference |
|---|---|---|
| seeking alpha / motley fool | editorial opinion, not structured signal scoring | we score signals, not form opinions |
| manual research | 1-2 hours per stock before earnings | brief is assembled and waiting 48 hours out |
| bloomberg / refinitiv | institutional pricing, more than needed | right-sized for operators and small RIAs |

## what makes it sticky
- consistent format means the user learns to trust it across many earnings cycles
- weekly watchlist brief creates a regular touchpoint even in off-earnings weeks
- signal accuracy over time becomes a feature — users can see whether the scoring predicted direction
- for newsletter operators: the brief is a writing scaffold, not just a reference

## one-liner for acquisition
"never show up to earnings unprepared again."
